About Us

✻

Our aim at Wellbeing 1st is to organise educational retreats for young people suffering with mental health problems or who simply wish to gain greater enjoyment from life. The social enterprise company was set up by a recent Cardiff University graduate, who believes in the power of yoga and meditation to allow people to heal themselves and achieve self-actualisation. The company was founded in September 2017.

There are currently two retreat offerings available to you...

Retreat Offerings

A weekend one day retreat that runs from 0930-1530 based in Cardiff

The day will consist of:

A yoga class (mindful movement)
A presentation on the importance of self-care to cover sleep, food and exercise
A meditation session
A wholesome vegetarian lunch

This program is subject to change depending on the requirements and interests of the group.
